Top Three Favorite NBA Finals Memories

2005 NBA Finals

This is the first NBA Finals I ever watched fully from game 1 to game 7. The Detroit Pistons faced off against the San Antonio Spurs and were hoping to repeat as NBA champions. However, the Spurs stopped them in their tracks. I was a neutral fan, but my brother was an avid Spurs fan so it was fun watching the games with him as he cheered or screamed at the televion.

2006 NBA Finals

When the Miami Heat went down 0-2 in the finals against the Dallas Mavericks, I thought it was over for the Heat. The Mavs were favored to win against the Heat yet one man said no to that: Dwyane Wade. He stepped it up and wound up carrying the Heat to four straight victories. This series had drama, surprises, and clutch performances; everything you want in a series.

2008 NBA Finals

This Finals featured the Boston Celtics vs the Los Angeles Lakers. This season Kevin Garnett and Ray Allen joined Paul Pierce and the Celtics and became the first modern superteam. All three hadn't had much success previously and to see them finally win a ring made me happy. I still remember KG screaming emotionally "Anything is Possible!" at the top of his lungs.

I've been watching the NBA for about a decade and a half and have so many positive memories to choose from. Today I was feeling a bit nostalgic about the NBA so this list reflects that. However, I still think these would be near my top memories even without my nostalgia glasses on.
